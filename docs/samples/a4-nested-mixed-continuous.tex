after the display must keep normal spacing and must not collide with the
formula.
\[
  a_1f(v_1)+\cdots+a_rf(v_r)=0
  \quad\Longrightarrow\quad
  f(a_1v_1+\cdots+a_rv_r)=0.
\]
The paragraph after the display continues here. It should be readable on every
compiler and should not be pushed into the formula area or detached by a large
blank space.
